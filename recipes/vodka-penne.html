Penne Alla Vodka Recipe

Description

Penne alla vodka is a pasta dish of penne in a tomato and cream sauce with a small amount of vodka, and sometimes small meats and vegetables such as sausage, pancetta or peas. Sometimes other pasta shapes are used. The alcohol is said to intensify and accentuate flavors in the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 LB Penne
  • 1/4 LB Prosciutto (optional)
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, Sliced Thin
  • 3/4 Cup Cream
  • 3-4 Tablespoons, Vodka
  • 1 Can Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Stem of Basil
  • 1/4 Cup Parsley
  • 1/2 Cup Parmesan
  • 2-3 Tablespoons, Olive Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons, Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare garlic and prosciutto. Slice up the gralic thin and prosciutto into little strips.
  2. Get a pot of salted water on the stove for the pasta and a pan for the sauce.
  3. In the pan, add olive oil and the prosciutto, cooking at medium heat until it just begins to crisp up and then add the basil and garlic. Cook until garlic starts to brown.
  4. Turn the heat off and let sit for a minute. Add the vodka and turn the heat back on medium, cooking until reduced.
  5. Add the tomatoes and cook at medium heat until it reduces and becomes very thick.
  6. Add the cream and cook it on a siimmer for a few minutes.
  7. Cook the penne until it's al dente. Drain and transfer to the sauce. Add a tiny bit of pasta water.
  8. Drop in 1-2 tablespoons of butter and serve immediately with parm and parsley.
